Hi. I am Giriraj Kavi. Let's step into Mumbai together.

As a Mumbaikar, born and raised in the bustling capital of Maharashtra, it is now my pleasure to invite you in and explore my hometown.

My passion is to connect with people and offer a perspective of the Mumbai that I have grown up in, with all its beautiful eccentricities.

Together we can marvel as our senses come alive with festivities, music, rich indian cuisine and many more. Take a look below at what we will experience in the beating cultural center of India.

Gateway of India

Enter Mumbai through the iconic architectural monument to India's colonial past.

Authentic Indian cuisine

Expand your palate and fill your belly with India's vast selection of aromatic flavours.

Elephanta Caves

Venture through time and explore cave networks that have been home to mystics, yogis and buddhists for thousands of years.

Dhobi Ghat Laundry

See the Dhobi's of the world's largest laundry beat clothes with stones and use coal heated irons to clean and press Mumbai's clothes.

Hanging Gardens of Mumbai

Take a breath of fresh air amongst the growing sculptures of Mumbai's Hanging Gardens.

Travel in style

Hop into a rickshaw and get ready to beep your horn as we travel Mumbai's streets.

Dharavi slum

See how the world's largest slum has created a billion dollar economy making clothes and other items for high-street brands across the globe.

Spiritual festivals

Dance, cheer and sing along to India's rich spiritual traditions and festivals that take place throughout the year.

Tea at the Taj

Kick back and enjoy a High Tea at the 5 star Taj Hotel.

Catch me if you can

Jump on a train and see if you've got what it takes to travel with the locals.

Let's connect

Get in touch and let's discuss your trip to Mumbai.
